About Us

Panimalar Engineering College, located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, is a premier institution offering top-quality education in engineering and technology. Affiliated with Anna University and accredited by the NBA, the college provides undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. Known for its excellent infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong emphasis on research, Panimalar Engineering College fosters an environment of academic excellence and innovation. The college also focuses on holistic student development through extracurricular activities, industry collaborations, and skill development programs, preparing students for successful careers and leadership roles in their respective fields.
